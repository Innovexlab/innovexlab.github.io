Our Diagnostic Panels

UTI Panel

Molecular detection of uropathogens including E. coli, Klebsiella, and Enterococcus.

Respiratory Panel

Screening for viral and bacterial respiratory pathogens including Influenza A/B, RSV, and Mycoplasma pneumoniae.

STI Panel

NAAT-based detection of Chlamydia trachomatis, Neisseria gonorrhoeae, and Trichomonas vaginalis.

Wound Panel

Identification of aerobic and anaerobic bacteria including MRSA and Pseudomonas aeruginosa.

GI Panel

Detection of enteric pathogens such as Salmonella, Shigella, and Clostridioides difficile.

Nail Fungus Panel

PCR-based detection of dermatophytes including Trichophyton rubrum and Candida species.

Antibiotic Resistance Panel

Screening for resistance genes including ESBL, carbapenemase, and MRSA markers.

Women’s Health Panel

Vaginal microbiome assessment including Gardnerella vaginalis, Atopobium vaginae, and Candida species.
