Advanced Pathogen Testing in Gainesville, FL

InnovexLab is a high-quality clinical laboratory specializing in molecular and microbiological testing for infectious diseases. Our Gainesville-based facility supports healthcare providers with accurate, timely results across a focused menu of diagnostic panels.

From urinary tract and respiratory infections to complex women’s health and antimicrobial resistance, InnovexLab delivers precision diagnostics with responsive service.

Our Diagnostic Panels

  • UTI Panel: Molecular detection of uropathogens including E. coli, Klebsiella, and Enterococcus. Supports diagnosis of uncomplicated and recurrent urinary tract infections.
  • Respiratory Panel: Comprehensive screening for viral and bacterial respiratory pathogens including Influenza A/B, RSV, and Mycoplasma pneumoniae.
  • STI Panel: NAAT-based detection of Chlamydia trachomatis, Neisseria gonorrhoeae, Trichomonas vaginalis, and Mycoplasma genitalium.
  • Wound Panel: Identification of aerobic and anaerobic bacteria including MRSA and Pseudomonas aeruginosa for skin and soft tissue infections.
  • GI Panel: Detection of enteric pathogens such as Salmonella, Shigella, Campylobacter, and Clostridioides difficile.
  • Nail Fungus Panel: PCR-based detection of dermatophytes and non-dermatophyte molds including Trichophyton rubrum and Candida species.
  • Antibiotic Resistance Panel: Screening for resistance genes including ESBL, carbapenemase, and MRSA markers to guide antimicrobial therapy.
  • Women’s Health Panel: Vaginal microbiome assessment including Gardnerella vaginalis, Atopobium vaginae, Candida species, and STI co-testing.
