Our Diagnostic Panels

UTI Panel

Molecular detection of uropathogens including E. coli, Klebsiella, and Enterococcus. Supports diagnosis of uncomplicated and recurrent urinary tract infections.

Respiratory Panel

Comprehensive screening for viral and bacterial respiratory pathogens including Influenza A/B, RSV, and Mycoplasma pneumoniae.

STI Panel

NAAT-based detection of Chlamydia trachomatis, Neisseria gonorrhoeae, Trichomonas vaginalis, and Mycoplasma genitalium.

Wound Panel

Identification of aerobic and anaerobic bacteria including MRSA and Pseudomonas aeruginosa for skin and soft tissue infections.

GI Panel

Detection of enteric pathogens such as Salmonella, Shigella, Campylobacter, and Clostridioides difficile.

Nail Fungus Panel

PCR-based detection of dermatophytes and non-dermatophyte molds including Trichophyton rubrum and Candida species.

Antibiotic Resistance Panel

Screening for resistance genes including ESBL, carbapenemase, and MRSA markers to guide antimicrobial therapy.

Women’s Health Panel

Vaginal microbiome assessment including Gardnerella vaginalis, Atopobium vaginae, Candida species, and STI co-testing.
