About InnovexLab

InnovexLab is a Gainesville-based clinical laboratory focused on advanced pathogen testing for physicians, clinics, and healthcare organizations. Our goal is to combine reliable laboratory science with practical, responsive service that supports day-to-day patient care.

We operate with a strong commitment to quality, traceability, and clear communication. With a focused test menu and streamlined processes, InnovexLab helps providers get the answers they need to make informed decisions for their patients.

Our mission

Our mission is to deliver accurate, timely pathogen testing that supports confident clinical decisions and better outcomes for patients. We prioritize:

  • Clear, clinically meaningful test panels
  • Consistent quality and laboratory best practices
  • Efficient turnaround times and reliable reporting
  • Responsive support for healthcare providers

Testing focus

InnovexLab focuses on infectious disease and pathogen testing, including UTI, respiratory, STI, wound, GI, nail fungus, antibiotic resistance, and women’s health panels. Our panel design is intended to align with real-world clinical needs and support antimicrobial stewardship.

Our service approach

We work to make laboratory testing straightforward for providers and staff. From onboarding and ordering to results delivery, InnovexLab emphasizes communication, consistency, and support. Our team is available to discuss panels, results, and general testing questions.
